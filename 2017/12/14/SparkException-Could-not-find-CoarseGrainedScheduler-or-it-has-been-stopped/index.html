SparkException: Could not find CoarseGrainedScheduler or it has been stopped.

2017-12-14
Spark
  1. 运行异常
  2. 解决

运行异常

  • 我在Python脚本中通过spark-submit提交运行多个任务（脚本中有多个spark SQL任务），任务正常运行完后报错
    ERROR netty.Inbox: Ignoring error
    org.apache.spark.SparkException: Could not find CoarseGrainedScheduler or it has been stopped.
  • 具体异常如下

解决

设置：spark.dynamicAllocation.enabled=false

  1. 可以在提交任务的时候设置：spark-submit –conf spark.dynamicAllocation.enabled=false
  2. 也可以在代码中通过SparkConf设置：conf.set(“spark.dynamicAllocation.enabled”,”false”)
